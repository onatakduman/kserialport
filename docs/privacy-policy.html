Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 23, 2026

Onat Akduman ("we", "us", or "our") operates the KSerialPort mobile application (the "App"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our App.

Information Collection and Use

KSerialPort is a serial port communication tool. The App does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information to our servers. All serial communication data stays on your device.

Data Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Serial port connection settings (baud rate, data bits, etc.)
  • Recent connection history
  • Custom macros and connection profiles
  • App preferences (theme, display settings)
  • Terminal log exports (saved to device storage)

Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services that may collect information:

Google AdMob

We display advertisements through Google AdMob in the free version of the App. AdMob may collect and use data to serve personalized ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information.

You can remove all advertisements by purchasing the Pro upgrade within the App.

Google Play Billing

In-app purchases are processed through Google Play Billing. Payment information is handled entirely by Google. We do not have access to your payment details.

USB and Serial Port Access

The App requires USB Host permission to communicate with serial devices connected to your Android device. This access is used solely for serial communication and no data is transmitted externally.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
